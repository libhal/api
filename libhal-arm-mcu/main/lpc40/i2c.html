class i2c : public hal::i2c#

i2c driver for the lpc40 series of microcontrollers

The lpc40 series i2c peripherals utilize a state machine and interrupts to handle transmitting and receiving data. This driver does the same. A hal::io_waiter may be passed to the constructor in order to control what the driver does when its waiting for the i2c transaction to complete.
